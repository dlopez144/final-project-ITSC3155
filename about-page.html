About the Healthy World Map

Our Vision

In a time when world health is taken into heavy consideration having easy and convenient access to world health information should be a must. We created Healthy World Map to allow everyone to have access to this information quickly and easily. Our vision is to allow those travelling to other countries, moving to other countries or anyone in need of this kind of data an easy place to access and visualize it with just a few clicks.

When using the World Healthy Map all that needs to be done is simply select the Continent you are interested in learning about and select the type of data you wish to visualize. You will be presented with a graph showing comparisons of each country in the continent you selected!

Looking Toward the Future

If future development of this project were to continue we would like to widen our scope and increase the detail of what can be visualized. One of our goals would be the visualization of specific countries as well as other features like comparing two or more specific countries or countries from other continents together.

Data Sources

All data sources are gathered from the World Health Organization
